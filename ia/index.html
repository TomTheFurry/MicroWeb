Shapes will be shown before the game starts, for a few seconds, and you can choose to skip it after watching.
After the game starts, the upper right corner will display the Shape or color you need to find.
If you find it successfully, you will get 10 points. You need to answer 4 questions correctly in each level to pass.
After completion, you will enter the next level.
The goal is to get more points before the game is over.

End Condition

Total error more than 3 times
* There will be a brief prompt after the error, showing the position of the Shapes and colors
